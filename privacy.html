Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025

Data We Collect

When you connect your Strava account, we access your basic profile information and cycling activity data (distance, duration, elevation, date). We only request read access — we never modify or delete your Strava data.

How We Use Data

  • To calculate daily/weekly ride stats
  • To identify members who cycle daily or at least 4 days a week
  • To build club leaderboards and felicitate consistent riders

Data Storage

We store minimal ride statistics (not full activity details) in a secure club-only database/Google Sheet. Data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Your Control

You can revoke Strava access at any time from your Strava account settings (“My Apps”). Once revoked, no new data will be collected.
