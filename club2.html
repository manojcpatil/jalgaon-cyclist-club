About Our Club

We are a community of passionate cyclists in Jalgaon. Our mission is to promote cycling as a healthy, fun, and eco-friendly lifestyle. Using Strava, we track rides and felicitate members who cycle daily or at least 4 days a week.

Why Strava Integration?

With our Strava-powered app, we can fairly measure member participation, create leaderboards, and recognize the efforts of consistent riders. This encourages more people to ride and builds a stronger cycling culture in Jalgaon.
